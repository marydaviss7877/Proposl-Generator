## Problem
Client needed a complete brand identity for their US-targeted dropshipping store. They had no visual identity and were losing credibility compared to competitors.

## Solution
Delivered full logo suite, color palette, typography guide, brand guidelines document, and social media kit. Included primary logo, icon mark, and dark/light variations.

## Results
Client landed 3 supplier partnership deals within 2 weeks of rebrand. Received 5-star review on Upwork and returned for a second project.
